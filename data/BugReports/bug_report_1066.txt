URL bar text sometimes cleared when pressing near url bar

Steps to reproduce
1. open focus
2. With url bar focused, enter two words ("master chief")
3. Click to move your cursor between the two words
4. Click the system status bar
5. Repeat moving cursor and clicking system status bar

Expected behavior
Text stays

Actual behavior
Text clears

Device information
Android device: Pixel 2
Focus version: 7.0, local build from a few days ago

In a more practical example, this happens to me when I moved the cursor to adjust what I typed. I've seen similar behavior in previous versions of Focus.
